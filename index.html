About Me

Hello, I’m Antara, a current senior and International Baccalaureate Diploma Candidate at Upper St. Clair High School. I'm interested in pursuing a degree in International Relations and Policy. My dream would be to work in the United Nations one day!

Now more than ever, we’ve seen the importance of social solidarity, empathy, and love. I'm an advocate for social change and a firm believer in the power of representation. After all, women belong in the house... and the Senate.

I'm currently involved in the Women and Girls Foundation GirlGov program, where I work on racial justice campaigns and advocacy. I also enjoy being a part of the National Speech and Debate Honor Society , Future Business Leaders of America, and Natural Helpers at school. When I'm not busy, you can catch me performing Indian classical dance or finetuning my K-Pop lip-syncing. Please check out my portfolio for some of my work!

I also recently gave a talk at TEDxYouth@UpperStClair about code-switching, which you can find below.
